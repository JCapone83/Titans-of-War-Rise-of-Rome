# Act XII Implementation Report

## Scope

Act XII carries the playable campaign from the complete AD 96 Imperial Capital judgment through the explicit AD 96-117 Trajanic continuation. The act ends at Trajan's death in AD 117. The Hadrianic Pantheon is deliberately outside the campaign chronology; the Pantheon visible today belongs to the next reign and should not be credited to the Trajanic capital.

## Player-Facing Systems

- The AD 96 outcome offers an explicit **Continue to Trajanic Capital** action rather than silently extending a finished campaign.
- A transition records the inherited capital, frontier, treasury, provincial, constitutional, and maintenance obligations.
- The Trajanic Capital panel exposes succession, constitutional continuity, frontier command, provincial trust, treasury resilience, conquest dependence, supply, provision, administration, maintenance capacity, and maintenance debt.
- Five operating-system scores keep territorial and monumental success separate from recurring administration.
- Six works are available through historically legible council prerequisites: the integrated Forum complex, administrative complex, Baths of Trajan, Aqua Traiana, Trajanic Portus, and the inherited Circus completion.
- The AD 117 outcome and exported chronicle record the operating form, score, public works, and continuing burdens.

## Historical knowledge

The opt-in Historical Knowledge drawer now covers all six Act XII turns. Its entries distinguish near-contemporary praise, later narrative, coins, inscriptions, monuments, archaeology, and administrative continuity. The Dacian wars are treated as both a victory and a ledger of frontier, veteran, provincial, and treasury obligations. Uncertain claims, including the precise AD 117 adoption sequence, are identified as uncertain rather than converted into game facts.

Keyboard focus enters the drawer, remains within it while open, returns to the invoking control on close, and marks a note consulted on either pointer hover or keyboard focus. Consulted notes are included in the exported campaign chronicle; unopened notes are not.

## Verification Standard

Acceptance requires complete turns 71-76, four viable reference strategies, full deterministic tests, the balance gate, the production build, six evidence-qualified historical entries, reachable UI continuation, and an AD 117 export. No Act I-XI outcome, chronology, project, or continuation rule is replaced by this work.
