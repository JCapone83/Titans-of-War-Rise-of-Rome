# Act VI Opening Balance Report

The optional Mediterranean opening runs from 264 BC through 241 BC.

- Turn 30 acquires bounded fleet capacity without assuming a mature naval state.
- Turn 31 prices maritime learning, losses, contractor exposure, and allied exhaustion.
- Turn 32 closes the First Punic War opening with war credit, Sicilian settlement, provincial trust, and imported grain visible in separate ledgers.
- Three continuation strategies resolve all councils with distinct choices and no later Punic wars, imperial systems, or late-Republic politics.
